# SHRM Human Capital Benchmarking & Talent Acquisition Reports — 2015–2025

## 2016 Report (FY2015 Data) — Human Capital Benchmarking Report

**Data period:** Fiscal year 2015
**Methodology:** Random sampling of SHRM members; 2,048 total respondents (1,503 answered succession planning; 1,192 addressed time-to-fill)

### Key Statistics
- **Average Cost-per-Hire: $4,129** (fiscal year 2015)
- **Average Time-to-Fill: 42 days** (stable benchmark from this period)
- 61% of surveyed organizations offered tuition reimbursement programs
- Average maximum tuition reimbursement benefit: $4,000 annually
- Only 6% of employees actually participated in tuition reimbursement programs
- **Executive hires are nearly 7x more expensive** than nonexecutive hires

### Cost-per-Hire Definition
Third-party agency fees + advertising fees + job fair costs + online job board fees + employee referral costs + travel costs + relocation costs + recruiter pay and benefits + TA system costs ÷ number of hires

## 2021–2022 State of the Workplace Report

### Key Statistics
- **68% of HR professionals agreed that finding and recruiting talent with necessary skills is a priority**
- Just **18% of in-person organizations reported being effective at finding and recruiting talent** vs. **46% of remote organizations**
- Only **32% of in-person organizations were rated as effective in retaining top talent** vs. 47% of remote organizations
- Over 80% of employees prefer to work for companies offering mental health support
- Only 38% of HR professionals said they effectively supported employees experiencing mental health challenges
- Finding and recruiting talent with necessary skills **increased 6 percentage points** from 2021
- Inflation was the fastest-growing HR concern in 2022, rising 13% from 2021

---

## 2022–2023 State of the Workplace Report

### Key Statistics
- Remote organizations significantly outperformed in-person organizations across both retention and recruitment effectiveness
- Inflation and mental health became top-tier HR concerns
- Top 2023 priorities: talent acquisition (68%), employee engagement, and mental health support

## 2025 Benchmarking Reports

**Published:** 2025 (updating 2022 benchmarking studies)
**Coverage:** 88 individual data sets spanning overall, industry groupings, sectors, organization sizes, and regions

### Key Updated Statistics
- Median time-to-fill (all roles): ~45 days
- Average cost-per-hire (non-executive): **$5,475**
- Average cost-per-hire (executive): **$35,879**
- **43% of organizations used AI for HR tasks in 2025** (up from 26% in 2024 — a 65% increase in one year)
