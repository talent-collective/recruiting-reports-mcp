# Bersin by Deloitte — Talent Acquisition Factbook 2015

## Spending

- **7% increase in talent acquisition spending** from 2013 to 2014
- **Average cost-per-hire: $3,999 in 2014** (nearly triple training spend per employee)

## Time-to-Fill

- **Average: 52 days in 2014** (up from 48 days in 2011)

## By Organization Maturity

| Maturity Level | TA Spend per Employee |
|---------------|-----------------------|
| Most mature (strategic) recruiting organizations | $6,465 per employee |
| Lowest maturity (reactive/tactical) | $3,258 per employee |

## Budget Allocation Trends (2011–2014)

| Channel | 2011 | 2014 | Change |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| Professional networking sites | 4% | 12% | Tripled |
| Agencies/third-party recruiters | 38% | 18% | Halved |
| Company websites | Top source | Top source | Stable |
| Professional networks (share of hires) | 10% | 10% | Unchanged |

- Company websites remain the top source of hires, followed by job boards and internal candidates
- Professional networks account for 10% of actual hires despite high spending growth

## Industry Data

| Industry | Note |
|----------|------|
| Healthcare | Spending increased 16% (highest among industries) |
| Healthcare new-hire turnover | 17% |
| Manufacturing new-hire turnover | 10% |

## Performance Impact

- **High-impact organizations achieve 40% lower new-hire turnover**
- **Strategic recruiting fills vacancies 20% faster** than tactical functions
