# Ashby — Recruiting Coordination Report

## Coordinator Staffing Trends

- Q1 2022 peak: ~4 RCs per organization
- End of 2023: ~2 RCs per organization (sharp decline)
- Companies <100 employees: average ~1.4 coordinators (relatively flat)
- Companies 100–500 employees: dropped from ~2.7 to ~1.7 coordinators (Q3 2022 onward)
- Companies 501–10,000+: sharpest drop; schedules per coordinator surged Q2 2023+

**Recruiter-to-Coordinator Ratios**
- Average: ~4 recruiters supported per coordinator
- Declined from ~5 (2020–2021) to ~4 (2022–2024)

**Weekly Scheduling Volume per Coordinator**
- 25th percentile: 14 schedules/week
- 90th percentile: 47 schedules/week

---

## Who Creates Schedules

- Recruiters: **70%** of all schedules created
- Recruiting Coordinators: **26.1%**
- Hiring Managers: **3.7%**

By interview type:
- Single-event: Recruiters 76% | RCs 21%
- Multi-event (panels): Recruiters 21% | RCs 67%

---

## Scheduling Speed Benchmarks

- **50%** of candidates provide availability within 24 hours of request
- **~65%** complete first interview within 1 week of availability request
- Median: **6 days** from availability request to interview start (incl. weekends)
- Last 8–10% linger beyond 2 weeks

**Single vs. Panel Scheduling**
- Single-event: 54% scheduled within 1 day of availability request
- Panel interviews: 34% scheduled within 1 day

**Direct Booking Links Impact**
- With direct booking: **72%** of single-event interviews scheduled within 1 day
- Without direct booking: 54%
- **33% faster** scheduling with automated direct booking

---

## Ashby vs. Legacy ATS Scheduling Speed

- Ashby: **4 days** from event creation to scheduled interview
- Legacy ATS: **5.6 days**
- Ashby is **~40% faster** (~1.5 days faster)

---

## Scorecard Submission Benchmarks

| Metric | Ashby | Legacy ATS |
|--------|-------|------------|
| Completion rate | ~90% | ~84% |
| Median submission time post-interview | 1.9 hrs | 2.3 hrs |

- Ashby: 7% higher completion rates; 17% faster submission
- Global average submission rates: 84–89% across platforms
- Majority of feedback submitted within 2 hours
- Metrics flat Q1 2021 – Q3 2024

---

## Interview Quality: Notice Period Effect

- **1-day notice interviews:** 68.2% pass rate
- **7-day notice interviews:** 63.2% pass rate
- Shorter notice may inflate pass rates — speed vs. quality tradeoff

*(Pass rate = scorecard scores of 3 or 4 on Ashby's 1–4 scale)*

---

## Organizational Structure Models

1. **Traditional RC Org** — dedicated coordinator team
2. **Distributed RC Org** — leaner coordinators, work spread to recruiters
3. **No RCs** — recruiters and hiring managers handle scheduling, aided by automation

---

## Coordinator Efficiency Insights (Named Practitioners)

- **Angelita Yarborough, Ironclad:** One-click availability saves 3–4 clicks/candidate; 10+ candidates = 10+ minutes/week compounded
- **Marizza Clemente, HackerOne:** Auto timezone conversion cut complex interview scheduling from 15 min → 5 min; eliminated time zone complaints
- **Veronica Salcido, Multiverse:** Automation frees coordinators for strategic work; 4–6 month coordinator-to-recruiter progression

---

## Key Conclusions

- Technology (direct booking, auto-scheduling) drives ~40% scheduling speed improvement
- Scorecard completion and speed improved with modern platforms
- Shorter notice periods correlate with higher (possibly inflated) pass rates
- Human judgment remains critical for sensitive candidate communications
