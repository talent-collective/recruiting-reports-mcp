# Gem — 2026 Email Outreach Benchmarks for Recruiters

## Data Foundation

- **6.2 million** email sequences analyzed
- **15.5 million** individual messages examined
- Source: 2025 Gem platform usage data

---

## Key Topics Benchmarked

1. **Sequence length** — optimal number of emails and structure
2. **Send timing** — best day and time by open rate
3. **Subject line strategy** — what drives opens, including token/personalization usage
4. **SOBO (Send On Behalf Of)** — hiring manager outreach effectiveness
5. **Company-size segmentation** — optimal sequence length varies by org size

---

## Market Context

- Open rates are declining across recruiting outreach
- Recruiter inboxes increasingly crowded
- Most teams lack benchmarking data to evaluate their own performance

---

## Key Conclusions

- At 6.2M sequences, this is one of the largest recruiter email datasets available
- Benchmarks enable teams to move from guesswork to data-driven outreach optimization
- Sequence structure and timing are independently optimizable levers
- SOBO (hiring manager as sender) is a measurable differentiator worth testing
