# Greenhouse — The Hire Standard: Recruiting Benchmarks 2026

## Methodology
Analysis of over 640 million applications across 6,000+ companies on the Greenhouse platform from 2022 to 2025. Data reflects North America unless otherwise noted.

## Key Findings

### Application Volume
- Applications per job increased 111%: from 116 (2022) to 244 (2025)
- Applications per recruiter increased 412%: from 146 (2022) to 746 (2025)
- Recruiters are now managing 411% more annual applications than in 2022

### Recruiter Team Size
- Recruiter headcount decreased 56%: from 10.43 per team (2022) to 4.62 (2025)
- Recruiting teams have been cut by more than half since 2022

### Recruiter Productivity
- Monthly hires per recruiter increased 122%: from 2.2 (2022) to 4.9 (2025)
- Jobs closed rate improved 8%: from 64.67 (2022) to 69.76 (2025)
- Despite half the headcount and 4x the applications, recruiters are hiring at more than twice the rate

### Time to Fill
- Time-to-fill increased 37%: from 43.64 days (2022) to 59.67 days (2025)

### AI Adoption
- Use of AI in the interview process is up 13 percentage points in the last six months

## Key Conclusions
- Smaller teams are handling exponentially more volume while still improving hiring rates
- The recruiter productivity gains suggest significant process and tooling improvements
- Time-to-fill rising alongside productivity gains indicates more rigorous hiring processes, not inefficiency
- AI adoption in interviews is accelerating rapidly

## Notable Quote
> "Recruiters work best when empowered by tools and processes."
