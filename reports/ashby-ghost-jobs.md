# Ashby — How Common Are Ghost Jobs?

## Job Fill Rates (2024, Ashby Customer Data)

- **82%** of open jobs resulted in actual hires
- **18%** remained unfilled, broken down as:
  - 5.5% — jobs paused
  - 1.0% — offer made but no hire occurred
  - 3.5% — no documented reason (potential ghost jobs)

---

## By Company Size

- Enterprise organizations: **89% fill rate**
- Smaller companies: higher variation in hiring outcomes

---

## Candidate Engagement

- **97.5%** of jobs at companies with 50+ employees moved at least some applicants to active interview stage
- Suggests most posted jobs reflect genuine hiring intent, even when unfilled

---

## External Survey Comparison

- Resume Builder survey (May 2024): "4 in 10 companies posted ghost job listings last year"
- Ashby's platform data shows a more conservative picture — most unfilled positions had documented candidate engagement

---

## Key Conclusions

- True ghost jobs (posted with no intent to hire) appear to be a small fraction of total postings in Ashby's data
- The 3.5% of jobs with no documented outcome are the closest proxy to ghost jobs in this dataset
- High candidate engagement rates (97.5%) suggest most postings represent real hiring efforts
- Larger companies fill roles at higher rates (89%) than the overall 82% average
