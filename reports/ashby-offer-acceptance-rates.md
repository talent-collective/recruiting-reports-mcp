# Ashby — Offer Acceptance Rates Report

## Overall OAR Benchmarks

- 3-year average OAR: **78%**
- 2021: 77%
- 2022: 76%
- 2023: **81%** (highest recorded)

**Key finding:** As days in offer stage decreases, OAR increases (inverse relationship)

---

## Time in Offer Stage

- Stable at ~3 days until Q2 2023
- Q3 2023 dip: dropped to ~2.5 days average (16% decrease)
- Candidates accepting: ~2.8 days (pre-Q2 2023) → fell to ~2 days
- Candidates declining: ~6 days
- Technical roles: ~4 days
- Business roles: consistent ~3 days

## Offer Declines & Rescissions

- Rescinded offers (technical roles): **17%** of declined offers
- Rescinded offers (business roles): **27%** of declined offers
- Peak rescission period: Q4 2022 – Q3 2023

---

## OAR by Source

- Highest OAR: Inbound applications (trending up since Q1 2023)
- Mid-tier: Candidate sourcing, referrals
- Lowest: Internal, agencies, external job boards

---

## Key Conclusions

- 2023 difficult hiring market correlated with faster acceptance times and higher OAR
- Technical candidates evaluate roles more selectively than business function candidates
- Inbound applications grew 2–3x from 2021 to 2024, driving more accepted offers
- Faster offer-stage decisions strongly predict higher acceptance rates
