# LinkedIn Future of Recruiting 2023

**Source:** LinkedIn Talent Solutions (business.linkedin.com/talent-solutions)
**Published:** 2023
**Methodology:** Interviews with global talent leaders; surveys of thousands of recruiting professionals; analysis of billions of LinkedIn data points; monthly surveys of 20,000+ LinkedIn members on job priorities. Regional editions for 10 geographies; industry editions for Healthcare and Staffing.
**Best for:** TA strategic positioning, skills-first hiring momentum, generative AI sentiment, DEI priorities, internal mobility, contingent labor growth

---

## Strategic Function

- **87% of recruiting pros say talent acquisition has become more strategic** over the past year
- 70% of TA professionals believe they can claim a seat at the executive table

---

## Compensation and Budget

- Only **45% of recruiting pros report companies increased salaries enough to match inflation**
- Compensation ranks #1 priority for candidates globally
- **53% of in-house recruiters predict recruiting budget will decrease or stay flat**
- 60% predict employer branding investments will increase

---

## Skills-First Hiring

- Recruiters are **+25% more likely to search by skills** than three years ago
- Recruiters are **+50% more likely to search by skills than by years of experience**
- **75% predict skills-first hiring will be a priority within 18 months**
- 94% agree understanding employee skills is necessary for informed talent decisions
- Only 64% can accurately assess candidates' skills today
- **20% of US job posts on LinkedIn don't require a four-year degree** (grew +30% over six months)

---

## Generative AI (February 2023 Sentiment)

- **68% were "very hopeful" or "cautiously optimistic"** about generative AI's recruiting impact
- 74% hope GAI automates repetitive tasks
- 67% want faster/easier candidate sourcing
- 59% want faster/easier candidate engagement

---

## DEI Hiring

- 69% say macroeconomic environment negatively impacts hiring
- **74% report DEI hiring is NOT being deprioritized** despite macro headwinds
- Nearly 20% say diversity hiring is now a higher priority

---

## Contingent Labor

- US contractor role job posts increased **+26% year-over-year**
- Full-time role posts grew only **+6% year-over-year**

---

## Candidate Priorities (Fastest Growing Year-over-Year)

1. Flexible working arrangements +5%
2. Compensation +4%
3. Supportive managers +4%
4. Clear goals from leadership +3%
5. Job security +2%

---

## Internal Mobility

- **Employees at companies with high internal mobility stay 60% longer**
- **75% of recruiting pros say internal recruiting will be important over the next five years**
- 81% say upskilling and reskilling will be important shaping forces
- 47% of L&D professionals report working more closely with TA this year
