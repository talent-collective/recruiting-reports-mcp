# Ashby — Candidate Sourcing Report

## Core Email Benchmarks

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Average email open rate | **86%** (stable throughout period) |
| Overall campaign reply rate | **19.6%** |
| Interest rate (of replies) | **37.7%** |
| Business roles reply rate | 18.7% |
| Technical roles reply rate | 19.4% |

- 2023 reply rates were 30–40% higher than 2022

---

## Sequence Structure

- **3-email sequences are optimal**
- 4+ emails plateau around 23% reply rate
- Response distribution (3-email campaigns):
  - ~50% reply to first email
  - Remaining split between 2nd and 3rd

---

## Timing

- **Best send window:** 12 PM – 6 PM local time
- Peak activity: 10 AM – 4 PM
- Reply rates remain stable across 24-hour period
- Saturday: lowest performance day

---

## AI Personalization Impact

| Condition | Reply Rate |
|-----------|-----------|
| Without AI personalization | 24.1% |
| With AI personalization | **35.3%** |
| **Lift** | **~46%** |

---

## Sourcing by Job Category

**Most sourcing investment:** Engineering  
**Least sourcing investment:** Customer Support  
**Highest reply rates:** Legal, Recruiting  
**Lowest reply rates:** Finance, Engineering  

**Inbound vs. outbound patterns:**
- Customer Support: highest inbound applications, lowest sourcing effort
- Sales: lowest inbound applications, highest sourcing effort

---

## Profile Review Efficiency

- 2022: Reviewed 5 profiles → added 4 to pool
- 2023: Reviewed 6 profiles → added 4 to pool (slight efficiency decline)

---

## Activity Trends

- Sourcing volume declined: second half 2022
- Bottom: first half 2023
- Uptick: second half 2023 (technical roles recovered sharper)

---

## Top Performing Teams (Named)

1. **Blacksmith Agency** — high open and reply rates; reviewed ~100 profiles per fill; ~5,000 for director-level roles
2. **Ramp** (Sonya Yeonas, Technical Recruiting Manager) — 3–4 stage sequences; doubled sourcing team in 2023; strong reply rates on 2nd or 3rd outreach
3. **Liveflow** (Kiera Cox, Technical Recruiter) — reaches 150 people/week with AI; candidates citing personalization quality as reason for response

---

## Key Conclusions

- Email open and reply rates stable over analysis period
- AI personalization drives ~46% lift in reply rates with less time investment
- Sourcing remains essential alongside growing inbound volume
- 3-email sequences are the optimal structure; 4+ show diminishing returns
