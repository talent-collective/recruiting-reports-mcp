# Jobvite Recruiting Benchmark Reports 2018–2019

## 2018 Recruiting Benchmark Report

### Key Metrics
- **Average time to fill: 40 days** in 2018
- Top-to-bottom funnel conversion rate: **~0.6%** (improved from 0.2% in 2016)
- Career site visitors required per hire significantly reduced from 526 (2016 baseline) to lower figure in 2018

### Source of Hire
- **Referral and recruiter-entered candidates account for 27% of hires** despite being a small portion of applicants
- Referrals and sourced candidates convert at dramatically higher rates than job board applicants

---

## 2019 Recruiting Benchmark Report

### Key Metrics
- Top-to-bottom conversion rate: approximately **0.6%** (consistent with 2018)
- Career site visitors per hire: reduced from 526 (2016) to lower figure in 2019
- Time-to-fill remained stable in the 2018–2019 period

### Quality of Hire Measurement
- **31% of recruiters measure success by quality of hire**
- 23% measure success by retention rate

### Benchmarking Categories
- Results segmented by company size, industry, and geography
- Enables peer comparison across funnel stages: awareness → apply → phone screen → interview → offer → hire
