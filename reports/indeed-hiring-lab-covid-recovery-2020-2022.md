# Indeed Hiring Lab — COVID-19 Impact & Job Posting Recovery 2020–2022

## COVID-19 Shock (Spring 2020)

| Date | Job Postings vs. Pre-Pandemic Baseline |
|------|----------------------------------------|
| March 15, 2020 | -2.9% (just beginning) |
| May 1, 2020 | **-39.3% below baseline** (peak shock) |

---

## Recovery Timeline

| Date | Job Postings vs. Feb 1, 2020 Baseline |
|------|---------------------------------------|
| May–July 2020 | Improved by avg. 1.6 ppts/week |
| June 18, 2021 | **+30.5% above pre-pandemic baseline** |
| July 16, 2021 | **+36.5% above baseline** |
| Late 2021–Early 2022 | **~60% above pre-pandemic levels** (peak) |

---

## Sector Highlights

| Sector | COVID Impact / Recovery |
|--------|------------------------|
| Hospitality & tourism | Down 47% in 2020 |
| Human resources | Among fastest recovering by October 2021; more than doubled during boom |
| Software development | More than doubled during 2021–2022 boom |
| Loading & stocking | Among fastest recovering sectors |
| Nearly all sectors | Above pre-pandemic baseline by mid-2021 |

---

## Key Benchmarks

| Metric | Benchmark | Period |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Peak job posting decline (COVID) | -39.3% | May 2020 |
| Peak post-pandemic hiring surge | ~+60% above baseline | Late 2021–Early 2022 |
| Weekly recovery rate (May–July 2020) | +1.6 ppts/week | 2020 |
| Sectors doubling from pre-pandemic | HR, software development | 2021–2022 |
