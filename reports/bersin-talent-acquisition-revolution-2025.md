**Source:** The Josh Bersin Company (in collaboration with AMS)
**Published:** September 16, 2025
**Data:** Global market analysis; case studies across multiple industries

## Market Size
- Global recruiting, hiring, and staffing market: $850 billion+
- Annual market growth rate: 13%
- 20%+ of employees consider changing jobs annually

## Current Hiring Challenges
- Only 17% of applicants reached the interview stage in 2024
- 60% of job seekers abandoned applications due to slow or complex hiring portals
- Less than 40% of candidates received regular communication from recruiting teams
- Over 25% of candidates rejected job offers due to poor hiring experience

## AI-Enabled Performance Improvements
- Time-to-hire acceleration: 2-3x faster with AI vs. non-AI peers
- 75% efficiency improvement in recruitment administration tasks
- 50% faster sourcing speed with AI tools

## Case Study Results

**Major U.S. resort operator:**
- 423% increase in scheduled interviews within 12 months
- 85% reduction in candidate drop-off

**Foundever:**
- 95% candidate satisfaction rating across 200,000+ applicants in 6 months

**Global automotive company:**
- $2 million first-year savings from AI-powered scheduling

## Five Core TA Strategies for the AI Era
1. Data-driven hiring decisions over intuition
2. Recruiters functioning as strategic agents managing AI tools
3. Future-ready technology stack with open APIs
4. Targeted AI pilots in high-impact areas
5. Business metrics tied to enterprise outcomes

## Strategic Framing
AI is transforming TA from "handcrafted" processes toward data-driven precision matching — moving from a "stack" to an "ecosystem" approach. Only 20% of companies use skills insights for hiring; just 9% have a user-friendly skills-based internal talent marketplace.
