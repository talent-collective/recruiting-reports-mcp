# iCIMS Annual Workforce Reports — 2019–2024

## 2019 Data

**Data Source:** iCIMS proprietary platform data (3M+ users, 200M+ applications, 5.5M+ hires/yr)

### Technology Hiring
- **44% of tech hiring professionals say less than 25% of their technology hires had a college degree** in a related field
- **61% of tech hiring professionals agree a four-year CS degree alone does not prepare job seekers** for today's workforce
- Technology roles take average **61 days to fill** vs. **42 days for non-tech roles**

### Contingent/Gig Workforce
- **82% of contingent workers have at least one current contract job that is knowledge-based**
- **76% of gig workers find contract work exciting**; only 24% find it stressful

---

## 2021 Workforce Report

### Key Statistics
- **91% of surveyed organizations said they are hiring for new roles** in 2021
- **Out-of-state applications increased 34% year-over-year**; nearly **1 in 4 applications from out of state**
- 72% said up to half of their workforces were **internally redeployed** in response to the pandemic
- **84% were concerned their current strategies were not reaching diverse talent pools**
- Diverse hiring activity "led the rebound" in employment toward the end of 2020

---

## 2022 Workforce Report

### Key Statistics
- **Hiring was up nearly 60% since January 2021**
- **Job openings up 49% since January 2021**; applications up only **12%** (significant gap signaling talent shortage)
- Hires up **31% in March 2022** vs. March 2021
- Tech hires up nearly **20% (Jan–May 2022)** vs. overall hires up 14%

### Sector Highlights
| Sector | Change |
|--------|--------|
| Retail | Job openings +8% |
| Healthcare | Job openings +5%; applications down 11% vs. pre-pandemic |
| Technology | Job openings +4%; hires +20% |
| Healthcare opening activity | +56% at close of Q1 2022 |

### Pay Level Distribution
- Job openings for $100K+ positions up only **1%**
- Openings for jobs paying <$50K up **10%+**

## 2023 Workforce Report

**Published:** Late 2022 (for 2023 outlook)
**Data Source:** Platform data from 3M+ users

### Key Statistics
- Job applications up **1% since January 2021** (flat despite hiring surge)
- **One-third of people say they are currently looking for a new job** (late 2022)
- Data drawn from 3M+ global platform users, 200M+ applications, 5.5M+ hires annually

---

## 2024 Workforce Report

### Key Statistics
- **Overall time to hire: average of 18 days in 2024**
- **Applicants per opening for entry-level jobs grew 13%** between May 2023 and May 2024
- AI saving UK recruiters average of **2.5 hours per week**
- **99% of UK recruiters using AI in hiring processes**
- **69% of talent organizations globally using AI** (varying adoption by funnel stage)
- **41% of recruiters fear AI may render their roles obsolete**
- **98% express concern about candidates' use of AI** during the application process
- **43% of Class of 2024 respondents desire a long-term career path** with a single employer
- U.S. saw **5% increase in hiring year-over-year** (2023–2024)
- Manufacturing leads frontline openings at **10% growth**
