# Ashby — Does AI Notetaking Improve Feedback Forms?

## Scorecard Submission Speed

| Window | With AI Notetaker | Without |
|--------|------------------|---------|
| Within 5 hours | ~9% higher submission rate | baseline |
| Within 24 hours | **70%** submitted | **66%** submitted |

---

## Content Quality

- Scorecards with AI Notetaker contain **~44% more words** than manual notes
- Additional content includes transcribed specifics and direct candidate quotes

---

## Key Benefits

1. **Operational velocity** — earlier submission prevents scorecards rolling into next-day lag, cutting 12–24 hours of delay
2. **Interview focus** — interviewers stay engaged with candidates rather than managing note-taking
3. **Debrief quality** — leadership can review specific interview moments or candidate quotes without scheduling additional rounds
4. **Data security** — native compliance removes risk of sensitive data in external tools

---

## User Testimonials

- **Monte Carlo** recruiting leader: reduced manual note refinement time; improved attendance and feedback speed
- **Supabase** technical recruiter: native system integration and compliance features enable higher-quality, more consistent evaluations

---

## Key Conclusions

- AI notetaking produces measurable lift in both submission speed (6–9%) and content depth (44% more words)
- Faster scorecards improve candidate experience by eliminating unnecessary process lag
- Quality improvement comes from freeing interviewers to focus on conversation
