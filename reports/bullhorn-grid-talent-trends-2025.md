# Bullhorn — GRID 2025 Talent Trends Report

## Methodology
Online survey of 2,766 respondents via third-party service (confidential responses), conducted June-July 2025. Covers permanent placement, temp/contract/SOW models across all firm sizes. Supplemented by 5 qualitative industry professional interviews.

## Recruiter Adoption Trends
- 35% of candidates use recruitment firms (down from 45% prior year)
- 66% prefer LinkedIn, Indeed, or direct job boards
- Professional candidates: 40% use recruiters
- Commercial candidates: 33% use recruiters
- Healthcare candidates: 30% use recruiters
- Gen Z candidates: only 26% use recruiters

## AI in Candidate Experience
- 50% of talent interacted with AI during their job search
- 77% of those had a positive experience
- 88% rated AI interviews equal to or better than live interviews
- 73% had a positive experience with AI voice agents
- 93% of candidates who had a positive AI interview experience continued working with their recruiter (vs. 76% overall)

## Candidate Attrition Drivers
- 75% stopped working with a recruiter due to lengthy process or poor communication
- 16% never received application confirmation
- 54% cited slow process or insufficient communication as top reasons for leaving

## AI Time Savings
- AI implementation could save 17 hours per recruiter per week
- Search-specific tasks alone: 4.5 hours weekly savings
- Automated screening tasks: 3.6 hours weekly savings

## Candidate Loyalty Drivers
| Factor | Loyalty Rate |
|--------|-------------|
| Positive AI experience | 45% increase |
| General AI use | 30% increase |
| Automated onboarding | 85% continue working with recruiter |
| Frequent communication (more than weekly) | up to 80% higher loyalty |
| Upskilling/reskilling participation | 91% remain loyal (vs. 63% without) |
| Working desired hours | 87% loyal (vs. 68% wanting more hours) |

## What Candidates Value in AI Interactions
- 32% value immediate response to applications
- 27% cite speed through process
- 13% appreciate knowing where they stand
- 10% note AI helps assess fit for role
- 54% of candidates moving through chatbot screenings appreciate immediate response

## Contingent Workforce
- 54% of temporary/contract workers want full-time positions within 2 years

## Communication Impact
- Dissatisfaction with interview communication: 40% overall, drops to 26% with AI voice agent use
