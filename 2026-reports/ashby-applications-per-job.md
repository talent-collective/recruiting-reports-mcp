# Ashby — Applications Per Job Report

**Source:** ashbyhq.com/talent-trends-report  
**Data:** ~13M applications (original); updated through January 2024  
**Period:** January 2021 – January 2024  
**Best for:** Application volume trends, first-week spike patterns, business vs. technical role comparison

---

## Volume Growth (Jan 2021 – Jan 2024)

| Role Type | Growth |
|-----------|--------|
| Business roles | **207%** (3x growth) |
| Technical roles | **161%** (2.6x growth) |

- Overall: application rates per week increased approximately **3x** across business and tech roles
- Peak volume: last week of August 2023

---

## First-Week Spike Pattern

- First-week applications are **2.5–3x higher** than all following weeks
- Strongest spike occurs in week 1 of posting, then drops to elevated steady state

---

## Job Categories Analyzed

**Business roles:** Customer Success, Customer Support, Finance, HR, Legal, Security, Marketing, Operations, Recruiting, Sales, Account Management

**Technical roles:** Engineering, Design, Data, Product Management

Notable: Support, Success, and Recruiting roles experienced the largest volume increases (2021–2023)

---

## Methodology

- Excludes referrals, sourced candidates, and non-direct application sources
- Focuses solely on direct applications through company websites and job boards
- Sample: predominantly US-based tech companies with 1–1,500 employees

---

## Recommended Management Strategies

1. Optimize job postings for signal-to-noise
2. Establish application review timelines
3. Set automated application thresholds
4. Develop filtering capabilities
5. Add application questions to screen intent
6. Leverage social channels for targeted visibility
